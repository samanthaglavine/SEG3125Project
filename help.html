How do I update my contact information?

To update your contact information, please follow the following steps:
1. Use the navigation bar on the left-hand side and click on the " Settings" option.
2. Update your contact information using the fields provided.
3. Click "Submit". *Note: An update confirmation will appear if the update was succesful.

How do I change my password?

To change your password, please follow the following steps:
1. Click the in the top right-hand corner.
2. Click on "Change Password"
3. Fill out the required information.
4. Click "Submit". *Note: An update confirmation will appear if the update was succesful.
